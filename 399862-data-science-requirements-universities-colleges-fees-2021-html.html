Data Science course: requirements, universities and colleges, fees 2021

Data Science course is becoming a competitive vocational program with the rising demand for experienced data analysts and scientists. The latest trends indicate that those who have superior data tapping and processing capabilities rule the world of business and technology. Those throwing a wishful eye on this lucrative course can now enroll in various institutions.

A good number of local universities and colleges offer the best Data Science courses and training. However, new students do not often realize which institutions they should join to train at affordable rates. This definitive guide explores all the requirements of completing a Data Science course in Kenyan higher learning institutions.

What is Data Science course?

This is a learning program that blends mathematics, business acumen, tools, algorithms, and machine learning techniques to help discover the hidden patterns or insights from raw data. Simply put, it is extracting knowledge from various types of data. Such information is beneficial in the formation of big business decisions.

Data Science courses in Kenya

Where can I study Data Science in Kenya? There are many Data Science courses in Nairobi and other leading towns in Kenya. Even so, it is essential to confirm that the institution you are enrolling in has the necessary accreditation to offer the program. Some of these provide free Data Science course training remotely.

1. Data Science course at Strathmore University

The institution acknowledges how data-rich modern businesses are becoming. For this reason, Strathmore aims to graduate experienced data analysts to help make data-driven decisions.

The school offers this interdisciplinary course to sharpen learners with computational, data management, and substantive statistical expertise. Qualified data scientists can flexibly work in different institutions, including law, government, administration, and business.

A certificate in Data Science is relatively affordable, with full-time classes and evening classes as an option. Intakes into the course take place in January, May, and September.

How do I become a data scientist in Kenya?

Who is eligible for data science course? Becoming a data scientist requires statistics, social sciences, computer science, and physical science qualifications. Merits in Data Science is another excellent way of standing out as a data scientist.

What subjects are needed to become a data scientist? Data Science requirements vary greatly across training centres. Overall, one should have an excellent mathematical background with excellent skills in statistics and computational methods.

Both local and international colleges and universities guarantee the best Data Science training. Luckily, minimum requirements and payable fees for Data Science courses are mostly manageable among most learners. Take a step today and start your career journey of becoming a data scientist.

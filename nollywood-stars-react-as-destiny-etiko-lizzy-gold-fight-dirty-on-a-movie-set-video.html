Nollywood Stars React As Destiny Etiko, Lizzy Gold Fight Dirty On A Movie Set (Video)

Nollywood stars, Nkechi Blessing and Uche Elendu have reacted to the video of Igbo-born script interpreter, Destiny Etiko in a fistfight with her colleague, Lizzy Gold.

This comes after a video capturing the moment the heavily endowed controversial script interpreter was seen confronting the outspoken actress, Lizzy Gold for going behind her back to visit a place she once took her to.

However, in a split second the actresses engaged in a fistfight, which led other colleagues on set to jump in and try to separate them.

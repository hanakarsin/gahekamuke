Nkechi Blessing Causes Stir As She Meets Ooni Of Ife

Nollywood Actress, Nkechi Blessing has caused a stir on social media after she shared a photo of her visit to the Ooni of Ife, Oba Adeyeye Enitan Ogunwunsi, in his palace at Ile-Ife, Osun State.

The controversial film star, who has been gushing about the Ooni and have countlessly expressed her desire to be amongst his numerous wives, could not contain her joy after meeting the monarch in person.

Sharing the photo on her verified Instagram handle, Blessing said that she had never laughed so hard before.

She also thanked the king for his warm reception

She wrote: ”ever met a jovial king? Well this picture says it all. I have never laughed this hard in a long time. Thank you so much for the warm reception his Imperial Majesty Ooni of Ife, Oba Enitan Adeyeye Ogunwusi Ojaja II. God bless your existence.”

Fans React

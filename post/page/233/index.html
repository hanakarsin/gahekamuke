Dino Melaye Gets Fresh Appointment

published on March 27, 2024
The People’s Democratic Party (PDP) has named former Kogi Senator, Dino Melaye as the Chairman of the Screening Committee for the South-West zonal executive congress. The Committee has the mandate to screen all aspirants for the zonal executive committee positions. The appointment was disclosed in a statement on Friday by the PDP National Organising Secretary, Col. Austin Akobundu (rtd.)

Professor Dora Akunyili Passes Away in India Aged 59

published on March 19, 2024
As family sources have disclosed to Sahara Reporters, former Director General of the National Agency for Food and Drug Administration and Control and ex minister of Information, Professor Dora Akunyili, has died today. She passed away at the age of 59 at an Indian Hospital where she has been receiving treatment for an undisclosed ailment. She was born Dora Nkem Akunyili in July 1954 in Makurdi, Benue State.

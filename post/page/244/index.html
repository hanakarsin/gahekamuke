Details Of Southern Governors' Meeting In Enugu Emerge

published on March 20, 2024
The details of the meeting of the Southern Governors Forum held in Enugu State on Thursday have emerged. The Governors who attended the meeting were Governors of Enugu State, Ifeanyi Ugwuanyi; Lagos State, Babajide Sanwo-Olu; Ondo State, Rotimi Akeredolu; Rivers State Nyesom Wike; Osun State, Adegboyega Oyetola; Delta State, Ifeanyi Okowa and Bayelsa state, Doye Diri.

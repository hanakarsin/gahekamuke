Military Bombs Boko Haram, ISWAP Commanders During Meeting

published on July 15, 2024
The Nigerian Military has bombed Boko Haram and Islamic State West African Province (ISWAP) commanders and leaders during a meeting around the Lake Chad axis. Naija News gathered that Nigeria Air Force jets targeted Boko Haram and ISWAP camps in Sabon Tumbu, Jibularam and Kwalaram. An officer told PRNigeria that the coordinated airstrikes destroyed the strongholds of the sects’ commanders and their guards. President Buhari Appoints New Minister

published on July 15, 2024
President Muhammdu Buhari has appointed Muazu Jaji Sambo as a new minister of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Naija News reports that Sambo is to replace the former Minister of Power, Sale Mamman, who was sacked by the President in September. In a letter to the National Assembly, President asked the Senate to screen and confirm the minister-designate from Taraba State.

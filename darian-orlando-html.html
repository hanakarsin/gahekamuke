Darian Orlando - Age, Family, Bio

Older sister of Johnny Orlando who has written original songs for Johnny to perform, such as "Summertime," "Replay," and "Never Give Up." She has also helped run her sister's YouTube channel where she has posted travel vlogs, makeup tutorials, challenges and Q&A videos. 

Before Fame

She started making videos when she was 13 years old and Johnny was 8.

Trivia

She edits all of her sister Lauren's videos. In January of 2020, she posted a photo with Shawn Mendes on her darianorlando Instagram account account. The photo has received over 39 thousand hearts. 

Family Life

She was born in Mississauga, Ontario, Canada to Meredith and Dale Orlando. Apart from Johnny and Lauren, she has a sister named Maddie.

Associated With

She went to see Austin Mahone perform in Miami for her 16th birthday.

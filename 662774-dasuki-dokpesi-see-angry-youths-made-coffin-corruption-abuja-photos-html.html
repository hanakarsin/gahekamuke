(PHOTOS): Angry Youths Make Coffin For Corruption In Nigeria

Some youths have taken to the streets of Abuja, Nigeria's federal capital, to lend their support to President Muhammadu Buhari in his fight against corruption.

The pictures obtained by Legit.ng showed the youths carrying different banners, where they showed their displeasure with some corrupt Nigerians.

Their grouse, Legit.ng gathered, is not unconnected to the recent revelations made by former National Security Adviser (NSA), Sambo Dasuki and founder of DAAR Communications, Chief Raymond Dokpesi, over the N2billion arms deal scandal.

[article_adwert]

The angry youths also made coffins, where they buried corruption and bid it goodbye. The #BringBackOurGirls agitators were also present at the venue of the protest and expressed their displeasure over the continued detention of the secondary school girls who were kidnapped over a year ago in Borno state.

Sunny Suljic - Age, Family, Bio

Skateboarder and child actor who appeared in the directorial debut of Jonah Hill, Mid-90s. He has also played Gabriel in 2015's 1915, Bob in 2017's The Killing of a Sacred Deer and Tarby Corrigan in 2018's The House with a Clock in Its Walls.

Before Fame

He made his acting debut in a 2013 short film called Ruined.

Trivia

He did voice work and motion capture for Atreus in the eighth installment of the video game God of War.

Family Life

He is from Georgia. He posted a picture to Instagram alongside his dad for Father's Day 2020. He has been romantically linked to Sissy Sheridan. 

Associated With

He had a minor skateboarding role in the Gus Van Sant-directed film Don't Worry, He Won't Get Far on Foot.

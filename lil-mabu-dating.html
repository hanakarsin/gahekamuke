Who Is Lil Mabu Dating? Details on the Rapper's Love Life

Rumors on social media suggest that rapper Lil Mabu is involved with this singer. A look at the rumors about the "Miss Me" artist's love life.

The Gist:

  • Rumors previously spread about Lil Mabu dating Nessa Barrett.
  • Lil Mabu's name was also linked to reality star and rapper Chrisean Rock after they teased a song together.

If you keep your ear to the streets, you likely know that rapper Lil Mabu (real name: Matthew Peter DeLuca) has been carve outing his lane. Although he entered the industry in 2019, it was his 2020 single “Miss Me” that helped him gain massive attention. Since then, the MC has collaborated with fellow musicians including Dusty Locane, DD Osama, and Chrisean Rock.

Now that he's in the public eye, folks have been wondering about his private life. Typically, celebs prefer to mix, mingle, and date in their own circles. And since the young artist has kept his romantic life private — and after rumors about a possible coupledom between him and Chrisean took over social media — fans want answers.

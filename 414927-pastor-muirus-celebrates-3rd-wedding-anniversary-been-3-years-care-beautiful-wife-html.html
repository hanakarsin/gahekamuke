City pastor Andrew Young and his wife, Edna Wairimu aka Nimo, have celebrated three years in marriage since their grand wedding in June 2018.

The couple's wedding was the talk of the town for days.

Young, who is the son of televangelist Pastor Pius Muiru married the love of his life in a colourful wedding ceremony. Young and Nimo were ferried in a limousine and a chopper to their wedding.

The couple was later blessed with one child a few months after their wedding and are happily married.

Celebrating their anniversary, Young shared photos of their happy moments accompanied by a sweet message to his wife.

"Its been three years of loving and taking care of my beautiful wife. Some say we look alike which I admit because she looks like me. All we can say is we thank God for this far and as we continue our prayer is that this marriage will bring glory to His name," he wrote.

Adding:

"Being guided by God and understanding we were not just brought together so that we can love each other only but also that we may fulfil a purpose God predestined in us. Flashy wedding

Young and his long time girlfriend Nimo formalised their union in a colourful wedding on Saturday, June 9, 2018.

It was a moment to behold for the two lovers both of whom come from very strong Christian background, the groom being son to seasoned televangelist Pius Muiru.

Dressed in pink bottoms, bridesmaids looked all elegant, and nothing could spoil the good day. After exchanging their vows, the newlyweds made way to Wilson Airport hangar for a quick photo shoot.

Tinubu Visits Senator Wamakko At His Residence (Photos)

The National leader of the All Progressives Congress, Bola Tinubu has met with the lawmaker representing Sokoto North senatorial district, Aliyu Wamakko, at his residence.

It was gathered that the meeting between Tinubu and the former Governor of Sokoto State was held at Wamakkoâ€™s residence in Asokoro, Abuja.

Wamakko while speaking during the visit expressed his appreciation to the former Governor of Lagos State for his kind gesture.

The lawmaker described Tinubu as a man of integrity for promoting peace and harmony among various political parties across Nigeria.

He also promised to support the presidential ambition of the APC leader ahead of the 2023 general elections.

Wamakko stated that Tinubu has the quality to rule Nigeria and make it a better nation for both the poor and less privileged.

This is coming after Tinubu visited President Muhammadu Buhari at the Presidential Villa, Abuja.

Speaking with State House correspondents, Tinubu had said he came to the Villa to thank the President for visiting him in London where he sought medical treatment.

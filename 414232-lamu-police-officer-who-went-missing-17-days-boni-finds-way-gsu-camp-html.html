Lamu: Police Officer Who Went Missing for 17 Days in Boni Finds Way to GSU Camp

- The officer identified as Police Constable Norman Mwongera Kiunga disappeared without a trace for 17 days

- According to a police report on Wednesday, June 2, the officer found his way to Basuba GSU camp

- He was evacuated for further medication after returning with two gunshot wounds

A police officer who went missing for 17 days in Boni enclave in Lamu county has found his way to Basuba GSU camp in stable but weak condition.

The officer identified as Police Constable Norman Mwongera Kiunga had disappeared without a trace and when he returned, there were cheers when his colleagues learnt he was still alive.

According to a police report on the Modern Corps page on Wednesday, June 2, Kiunga had two gunshot wounds.

"These included a bullet lodged in his thigh and a bullet wound at the area between the elbow and wrist," the report said.

Kiunga was evacuated for further medication.

Chris Kratt - Age, Family, Bio

Wildlife enthusiast and the creator, writer and producer of Zoboomafoo, Kratts' Creatures, and other popular educational shows.

Before Fame

He earned a B.A. in biology from Carelton College, Minnesota, in 1992.

Trivia

He received funding for his ecology studies from the Explorers Club and the National Science Foundation.

Family Life

He and his brother Martin started a new series, Wild Kratts, in 2011. He married Tania Kratt and together they had two sons.

Associated With

He and his brother hosted a TV series, Creature Adventures, that was set at Dolly Parton's amusement park, Dollywood.

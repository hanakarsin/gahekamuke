Kaylee Halko - Age, Family, Bio

Social star who was born with a rare, premature aging condition called progeria, which makes her young body age at a faster pace. Her personal Instagram account, where she posts pictures of her day-to-day life, has earned over 170,000 followers.

Before Fame

In 2010, she was featured in an episode of 20/20. 

Trivia

She first gained a web following on musical.ly, amassing over 5 million fans on her khalko account. She was bullied in school with fake Instagram pages. 

Family Life

She was born to parents Marla Lynn Halko and Timothy George Halko. She has an older brother named Jacob Halko.

Associated With

She and Adalia Rose are both social stars living with progeria.

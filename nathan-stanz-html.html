Nathan Stanz - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube star and streamer who is best known for his self-titled YouTube channel Stanz. He is known for posting commentary content and featuring Ludwig Ahgren in his videos. He has gained over 220,000 subscribers and over 58 million views. 

Before Fame

He began posting content on Instagram in September 2014. 

Trivia

In 2021, he was a model for an ad for Gen.G Esports. He is the co-founder of OFFBRAND. One of his most popular Youtube videos revolvers around funny British YouTubers. It has garnered more than 300,000 views. 

Family Life

His wife Richelle Stanz has appeared on his social media accounts. 

Associated With

In August 2022, he posted a photo alongside basketball player Chris Bosh.

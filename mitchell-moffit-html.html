Mitchell Moffit - Age, Family, Bio

Co-creator of the popular YouTube channel asapSCIENCE along with Gregory Brown. The channel has earned over 10 million subscribers. In 2012, he was also part of the Canadian channel ApprenticeEh. 

Before Fame

His entry into the 2008-2009 Best Job in the World promotion was shortlisted and ranked third amongst the 49 other shortlisted entries as of March 2009. 

Trivia

He and Greg's channel features popular videos like "Which Came First - The Chicken or the Egg?" and "Amazing Facts to Blow Your Mind Pt. 2." 

Family Life

He was born in Guelph, Ontario, Canada. His sister is fellow YouTuber Kimberly Moffit. He's openly gay and started dating Gregory Brown in the mid-2000s. 

Associated With

Bill Nye was featured in a video on his channel titled "Could We Stop an Asteroid?" Lilly Singh has also been a guest on the channel.

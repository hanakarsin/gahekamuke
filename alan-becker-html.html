Alan Becker - Age, Family, Bio

Content creator on YouTube with a channel that has earned 26 million subscribers. He is known for creating Adobe Flash stick figure animations and went viral in 2006 with his "Animator vs. Animation" series. He and Dj Welch would collaborate on a channel called Animators VS Games.

Before Fame

He posted his "Animator vs. Animation" video on the site Newgrounds where it went viral. He attended the Columbus College of Art and Design.

Trivia

In 2007, his "Animator vs. Animation II" won a "People's Choice" Webby Award. Spin-offs of his series have included "Animation vs. Minecraft" (AVM), "Animation vs. YouTube" and "Animation vs. League of Legends."

Family Life

He has a sister named Harmony Becker, AKA hamini on Instagram. He married @kaoriskitchen.

Associated With

He is known for his stick figure animations like Zig GamerX.

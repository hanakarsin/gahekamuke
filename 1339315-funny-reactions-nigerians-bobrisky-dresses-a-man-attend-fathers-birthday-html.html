Funny reactions from Nigerians as Bobrisky dresses as a man to attend his fathers birthday

- On June 16, 2020, interesting photos of popular Nigerian cross-dresser, Bobrisky, made the rounds on social media

- Bobrisky had attended his father’s birthday dressed as a man instead of as a slay queen like he has been known for

- Nigerians took to Twitter to react to the photos and they had a lot of things to say

The Nigerian social media space is no longer as hostile to men who want to showcase their femininity through their appearance. These days, there are several Nigerian men on social media whom people have grown to love despite their cross-dressing.

However, that has also not stopped them from having detractors but people have at least gotten used to seeing them dressed as women.

One of the leading cross-dressers in the country, Idris Okuneye aka Bobrisky, however, left a lot of people shell-shocked after recent photos and videos of him dressed as a man surfaced on social media.

The popular cross-dresser had gone to visit his father to celebrate the occasion of his birthday and he was dressed like a man in an understated black jalabiya and a black and red fez cap.

Bobrisky is known to be one who loves glitzy things as well as drawing attention to himself and it came as a surprise to many that he could abandon his femininity for a moment while going to visit his father for his birthday.

As expected, the photos and videos got Nigerians buzzing on social media and many people had funny things to say about it.

Some were of the opinion that the fear of African parents was the beginning of wisdom while others said that they wished every day was Bobrisky’s father’s birthday so that the cross-dresser would continue to appear as a man.

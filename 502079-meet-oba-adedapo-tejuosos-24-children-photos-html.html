The Osile is well respect not only because he is a monarch but because he is well read.  He was a Medical Doctor and a respected businessman before he became king.

He is one Yoruba monarch who is born again Christian. He is blessed with 24 children (12 daughters and 12 men). According to Kemi Filani, many of his children are married into prominent Nigeria families. One stricking thing about Oba Adedapo Tejuoso children is their striking and sexy eyeball. This most of them took from their father, Kabiyesi himself.

He became a medical Doctor at the young age of 21.He is married to the beautiful and ageless first daughter of Okoya Dynasty, Moji Okoya and they are blessed with many children.

He is also married to one of the daughter of renowned Nigeria Professor, Hon Funmi Tejuoso who use to be one of the honourable members of Lagos house of assembly representing Mushin.

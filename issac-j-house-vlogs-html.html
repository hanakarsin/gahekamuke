Isaac Johnston (YouTube Star) - Age, Family, Bio

YouTuber who is known for appearing on the family channel J House Vlogs. He has frequently appeared alongside his mother Kendra, his father Jeremy, and his siblings. 

Before Fame

He first appeared in his YouTube channel's first video, which was posted on September 18, 2014.

Trivia

He has helped the J House Vlogs YouTube channel amass more than 2.8 million subscribers. He has been homeschooled alongside his siblings. In 2021, he was given 10 thousand dollars by his parents and he chose to invest in Bitcoin and Ethereum. 

Family Life

He has frequently appeared in conjunction with his siblings, whose names are Elise, Caleb, Laura and Janae.

Associated With

He and fellow family YouTuber Gavin Butler are both known for first appearing on YouTube as children.

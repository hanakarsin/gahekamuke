Mia Swier - Age, Family, Bio

Writer and producer at Fox Networks who previously worked as a producer at Showtime Networks. She has worked on TV programs such as Saturday Night Live, Who Wants to be a Millionaire, The Tony Awards, the Super Bowl and more.

Before Fame

She worked for ABC, NBC and USA Network while attending New York University's Steinhardt School for Media Studies and Television Production. She then worked for several years at White Cherry Entertainment.

Trivia

She co-founded a film production company called Effin Media in 2009. As a musician, she has performed as a bassist and vocalist in bands called Shoot the Freak, Guns n' Hoses and Mad Moon Riot, the former two of which she started.

Family Life

Her stage name is Mia Von Glitz. Her father worked as a painter under the name Laszlo Von Glitz. She began dating actor Darren Criss in 2010; they became engaged in January 2018. She and Darren tied the knot in New Orleans in February 2019. In October 2021, they announced they were expecting their first child due in Spring 2022. The couple welcomed their daughter, Bluesy, in April 2022. 

Associated With

She did promotional content for the long-running series Dexter, starring Michael C. Hall.

Stop Destroying Womanhood Solomon Buchi Slams Bobrisky

Embattled relationship expert, Solomon Buchi has said that popular Nigerian crossdresser Bobrisky is promoting rotten values on social media.

The writer in a post on Twitter berated the Instagram influencer for justifying cheating and promoting modern runs.

He argued that Bobrisky is trying to destroy virtuous womanhood.

His outburst emanated from the crossdresser’s recent post where he claimed to be a side chick that loves his partner’s spouse and would ensure her husband never tramples on her interest.

He warned the Instagram celebrity to cease from telling single women that ‘there is a cool and kind way to date married men’ because such ideology is non-existent.

DJ Cuppy Celebrates As She Graduates From Oxford

Nigerian female disc jockey and billionaire’s daughter, Florence Otedola, popularly known as DJ Cuppy, has been applauded by many Nigerians for spilling the truth about her graduation from Oxford University.

Naija News reports that the ‘Gelato’ crooner took to the microblogging platform Twitter, to celebrate her latest achievement.

DJ Cuppy revealed she has received her thesis result, which classifies her MSc degree, however, she didn’t get merit or distinction but she passed.

According to the billionaire’s daughter, it was a tough year and she’s thankful to God for her graduation.

She tweeted: “Got my THESIS result, which classifies my MSc degree… I didn’t get a Merit or a Distinction, but I PASSED!!! After a tough year, I can officially say I am now a GRADUATE from

@UniofOxford And I am bloody PROUD of myself… To GOD be the GLORY.

“Before I forget… a special SHOUT OUT to my Cupcakes for supporting me throughout my degree! Oya, time to plan a huge GRADUATION PARTY”

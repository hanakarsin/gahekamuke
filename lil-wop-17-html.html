Lil Wop 17 - Age, Family, Bio

Atlanta rapper also known as The Wopster. He started making waves in the music industry after the release of his tracked titled "Lost My Mind." He signed to Gucci Mane's 1017 Records label in 2017.

Before Fame

He released his three-track project, Wopster The Mixtape, in December 2016.

Trivia

He tattooed Gucci Mane's infamous ice cream tat on the right side of his face.

Family Life

He's based in Atlanta. His cousin is rapper Famous Dex.

Associated With

His song "Lost My Mind" was inspired by his idol, Gucci Mane.

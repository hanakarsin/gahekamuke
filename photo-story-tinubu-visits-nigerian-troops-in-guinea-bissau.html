Tinubu Visits Nigerian Troops In Guinea-Bissau

President Bola Ahmed Tinubu on Saturday visited the Nigerian Troops stationed in Bissau as part of the ECOWAS Stabilisation Force.

Naija News reports that Tinubu visted the troops on arrival at the Aeroporto International Guinea-Bissau ahead of the 63rd Ordinary session of the Authority of Head of State and Goverment of the Economic Community of West African State ECOWAS.

He is accompanied by Force Commander ECOWAS Stabilisation Force, Brigadier General Al-hassan Grema, National Security Adviser, Nuhu Ribadu and Permanent Secretary, Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ambassador Adamu Lamuwa.

Exclusive: Comprehensive List Of Winners At 2015 AFRIMA

The second edition of the All Africa Music Awards (AFRIMA) was held at the Convention Centre of Eko Hotel and Suites in Lagos on Sunday, November 15.

Many African artists across the continent came out en masse to be a part of what is referred to by many as Africa’s Grammy. See list of winners below:

[article_adwert]

Best Female Artist in West Africa - Yemi Alade - Nigeria

Best Male Artist in West Africa - Olamide - Nigeria

Best Male in Southern Africa - Cassper Nyovest - South Africa

Best Female in Southern Africa - Busiswa - South Africa

Best Male in Northern Africa Ahmed Soultan - Morocco

Best Female in Northern Africa Manal - Morocco

Best Male in Eastern Africa - Diamond Platnum-  Tanzania

Best Female in Eastern Africa - Tsedenia - Ethiopia

Best Male in Central Africa - Stanley Enow - Cameroon

Best Female in Central Africa - Charlotte Dipanda - Cameroon

Best Female Artist in Inspirational - Betty Akna - Equatorial Guinea

Best Male Artist in Inspirational - Darey - Nigeria

Best Artist in RNB & Soul-  Praise - Nigeria

Best Artist in African Contemporary - Charlotte Dipanda - Cameroon

Best Artist in African Hip Hop - Cassper Nyovest - South Africa

Best Artist in African Electro - Flavour - Nigeria

Best Artist in African Pop -Vanessa Mdee - Tanzania

Best Artist in African Rock - M’vula - Angola

Best Artist in African Raggae, Ragga & Dancehall - Stonebowy - Ghana

Best Artist in African Jazz - Kunle Ayo - Nigeria

Most Promising Artist in Africa -Kiss Daniel - Nigeria

Video of the Year - Stanley Ajetey Wiyaala - Ghana

Revelation of the Year - Adekunle Gold - Nigeria

Album of the Year - Charlotte Dipanda - Cameroon

Artist of the Year - Diamond Platnum -Tanzania

Best African Collabo - AKA ft Burna Boy - South Africa/Nigeria

Producer of the Year - Sauti Sol & Cedric - Kenya

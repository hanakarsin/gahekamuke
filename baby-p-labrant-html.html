Posie Rayne LaBrant - Age, Family, Bio

Daughter of social stars Savannah and Cole LaBrant, who run the YouTube channel The LaBrant Fam. Her parents' YouTube channel has amassed over 13 million subscribers and 4.5 billion overall views. 

Before Fame

Her family's channel was originally called Cole&Sav prior to her birth. On May 9, 2018, her parents filmed themselves finding out that they were pregnant with her. 

Trivia

The video of her birth was uploaded to YouTube on December 29, 2018. In less than 5 days, it had amassed over 8.5 million views. 

Family Life

She has a younger brother named Zealand, a younger sister name Sunday and an older half-sister named Everleigh Soutas.

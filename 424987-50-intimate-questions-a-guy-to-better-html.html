50+ intimate questions to ask a guy to get to know him better

When you like someone or are at the beginning of a new relationship, one of the most exciting ways to get to know your crush is by asking questions. Sometimes it's hard to know what questions to ask your partner to get to know him better. Intimate questions to ask a guy should come in handy when starting such a conversation.

Communication and self-disclosure can help build intimacy in marital relationships. Simply taking the time to chat with your spouse or significant other can make your bond stronger. However, sitting down and just talking things through in a relationship is not enough. You need to be able to ask your partner the right kind of questions, including intimate ones.

Woman Wins KSh 4.5 Million Lottery Jackpot Days after Hubby Said It Was a Waste of Time

  • When a UK woman sought to enter a lottery, her husband discouraged her
  • Debby Gardiner's husband Graham told her that participating in the lottery was a waste of time
  • However, days later, she won millions, and they now plan on going on a family vacation

A man has been left red-faced over comments he made to his wife before she won a Lottery jackpot.

According to Graham Gardiner, a driving instructor, he told his wife Debby that participating in the lottery was a waste of time days before entering the draw.

Changing tune

However, days later, they received news that she had won KSh 4.5 million. On hearing about the win, the 60-year-old changed tune.

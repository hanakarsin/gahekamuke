Ebuka Obi Uchendu turns hairdresser (photo)

- Ebuka Obi-Uchendu plaits hair for wife

- He blames it on economy recession

- The TV personality and wife are expecting their first baby

TV personality, Ebuka Obi-Uchendu, do really know how to pamper a woman especially during this economy recession.

The Rubbin Mind anchor man on Channels TV did a Snapchat of himself braiding his wife, Cynthia’s hair. He gave her a professional cornrow style.

The event anchor revealed that the recent recession in Nigeria brought back his talent of hair making.

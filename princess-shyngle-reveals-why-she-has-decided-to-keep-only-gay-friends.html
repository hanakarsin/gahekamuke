Princess Shyngle Reveals Why She Has Decided To Keep Only Gay Friends

Gambian actress Princess Shyngle has revealed that she is considering keeping only gay friends.

The actress took to her Instagram account to make the disclosure to her followers.

She stated that the reason she was choosing to go with just gay friends was because straight people are envious, judgmental, and boring.

She wrote: “At this point in my life, I think I only wanna have gay friends.

“I’m sick and tired of y’all boring ass, judgmental, hating ass straight mofos.”

Recall that Shyngle had recently revealed that a man above 35 who is comfortable and not married is gay.

The actress maintained that the men would simply use the women in their lives, keep them hopeful and refuse to marry them.

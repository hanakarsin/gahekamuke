Tinubu Returns To Nigeria From UK [Video]

The President candidate of the All Progressives Congress (APC) Asiwaju Bola Ahmed Tinubu has returned to Nigeria.

Naija News reports that the former Lagos State governor returned to the country on Thursday after observing a 10-day rest in the United Kingdom.

He was received by a delegation led by his running mate Senator Kashim Shettima, former APC chair Adams Oshiomhole and others.

He alighted from the plane that conveyed him to a shout of ‘’Jagaban’’ and ‘’Asiwaju’’ in a video shared by the spokesman of his Presidential Campaign Council, Festus Keyamo.

Keyamo wrote: “He teased them; he played mind games with them; their thoughts went wild; but @officialABAT is the master of the game. Just when they thought they had something on which to feast, he’s rather the one now feasting on their naivety. Welcome back, the City Boy!”

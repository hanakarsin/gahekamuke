Sabrina Bahsoon - Trivia, Family, Bio

Trending social media personality and TikTok content creator who rose to fame through the use of her self-titled account. She went viral for her lip sync performances and skits that she recorded while traveling the London Underground. Her videos led to her receiving the nickname London's Tube Girl. She has more than 800,000 followers. 

Before Fame

She studied law at Durham University. She made her TikTok debut in the year 2020. 

Trivia

In September of 2023, her "So now im en route via tube. The man in the back getting a show fr" TikTok went viral with more than 11 million views in less than a week. She was featured on BBC News around the same time. After going viral she had the opportunity to appear at MAC Cosmetics' show during the London Fashion Show. 

Family Life

She was born in Malaysia but relocated to the United Kingdom for college. Her Dad is half Sierra Leonean and half Lebanese, and her mom is Malaysian.

Associated With

She created a TikTok set to Tate McRae's "Greedy."

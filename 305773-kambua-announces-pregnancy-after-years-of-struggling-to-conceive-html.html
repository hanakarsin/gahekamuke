Gospel singer Kambua announces pregnancy after years of struggling to conceive

After years of being trolled by haters who also labelled her barren, gospel singer and former Kubamba hostess Kambua has finally gotten pregnant, TUKO.co.ke has learnt.

An excited Kambua surprised her fans after announcing her pregnancy on her official social media pages.

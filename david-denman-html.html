David Denman - Age, Family, Bio

Widely known as Roy Anderson from the popular NBC television series The Office. He starred in the 2019 film Brightburn.

Before Fame

His first exposure was with Keanu Reeves in the movie The Replacements in 2000.

Trivia

He appeared in guest roles on television shows like Bones and Grey's Anatomy.

Family Life

He married Mercedes Masohn and was previously married to Nikki Boyer.

Associated With

He was in the drama ER with Anthony Edwards.

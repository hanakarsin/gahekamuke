Young Lyric - Age, Family, Bio

Rapper out of Houston, Texas who gained a large internet following after uploading her original songs to YouTube in 2010. She began starring on the Lifetime TV reality show The Rap Game. 

Before Fame

She recorded her first song at age 7, and would go on to perform it at the same age.

Trivia

She is known for claiming the titles of Hip Hop Princess and The Best 15-Year Old Rapper Alive. Her musical inspirations include Beyonce and Nicki Minaj.  

Family Life

Born Lyric Michelle Ragston, she was encouraged to pursue music by her father and uncle, who are both musicians. She grew up with sisters. She dated NBA YoungBoy. She began a relationship with boxer Shakur Stevenson in 2019. They got engaged in mid-2022. In October 2021, they revealed that they were expecting a daughter together. She gave birth to Leilani Asha on December 6, 2021. 

Associated With

She released a remix of Lil Wayne's song "No Worries" in 2013. She has also remixed hits by Drake, Kanye West and Wiz Khalifa.

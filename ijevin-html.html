iJevin - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube gamer who is known for his iJevin channel. He has gained widespread popularity there for his frequent Minecraft gameplay highlights and tutorial video posts. His YouTube channel has amassed more than 800,000 subscribers since its creation.

Before Fame

He began his YouTube channel on June 27, 2010. He posted his first video there on September 5, 2010.

Trivia

He has also operated his own iJevin Twitch Minecraft broadcasting channel, where he has earned more than 200,000 subscribers. His YouTube channel has become so popular that his videos on the platform will often rack up tens of thousands of views within their first few days of release. As well as Minecraft, he will also post gameplay from Hermitcraft to his many social media channels.

Family Life

He was born and raised in the United States.

Associated With

He and fellow YouTube gamer EthosLab are both known for their prolific Minecraft channels.

'Nah Your Body Go Tell You'

Nigerian award-winning singer, David Adeleke, popularly known as Davido, has expressed concern for a fan embarking on a journey from Taraba State to Lagos for a chance to meet with him.

Naija News reports that this comes after the Afrobeat singer told a fan cycling from Benue to Lagos to turn back because he is currently not at home.

However, another fan with the Twitter user name, Dami4reign, has revealed plans to journey 828 kilometres to see Davido in Lagos.

In the post shared via his Twitter page, the fan urged his followers to wish him luck and safety as he embarks on the journey.

He wrote: “My name is Dami, I just started my journey from Taraba to Lagos, which is about 828km, to see @davido. Wish me luck and safe journey guys”

Reacting, Davido retweeted the post expressing worry that the fan chose to make the journey by foot.

He wrote, “With leg, nah Your body go tell you”

Davido Drops Jaye Lo Official Video

Meanwhile, Davido, has dropped a fresh official video for DMW’s new signee Logos Olori’s debut single titled ‘Jaye Lo’.

Recall that Davido received backlash last month when he first released ‘Jaye Lo’ visuals on social media.

The award-winning singer was criticized by Muslims, most especially northerners, who found some scenes in the visual offensive.

But on Friday, Davido took to his verified social media pages to announce a new music video for ‘Jaye Lo’.

The new clip seen by Naija News showed Olori showing off his body and dance moves in the midst of some vixens.

It is worthy of note that Davido’s DMW took off the controversial scene from the initial video.

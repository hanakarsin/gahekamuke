SypherPK - Age, Family, Bio

Professional Fortnite player and Twitch streamer known online as SypherPK, who has played professionally for Luminosity. Aside from Fortnite, he has also streamed Battle Royale, Elder Scrolls Online, and For Honor, amongst other titles. 

Before Fame

He began his YouTube channel in January 2011. He began regularly streaming RuneScape in 2012. 

Trivia

He has amassed over 5 million followers on Twitch and more than 5 million subscribers to his YouTube channel. His September 2018 "Cutest Kid On Fortnite Sings Me A Song (& Makes My Wife Cry) (Fortnite Battle Royale)" video has received over 19.7 million views. 

Family Life

He's based in Austin, Texas with his wife Daniela. His real name is Ali Hassan. He has two younger brothers named Junior and Otto.

Associated With

He and Alex Ramirez are both famous YouTube gamers known for their Fortnite content.

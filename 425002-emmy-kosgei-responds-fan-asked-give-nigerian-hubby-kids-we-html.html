Emmy Kosgei Responds to Fan Who Asked Her to Give Nigerian Hubby Kids: &quot;We Already Have&quot;

  • Emmy Kosgei had taken to Facebook to celebrate her eighth marriage anniversary with her Nigerian husband Anselm Madubuko
  • However, Kosgei's fan identified as Betty Bayoti questioned her when she was intending to have children
  • The award-winning singer seemingly did not take issue with her question and responded to her gently

Celebrated gospel artiste Emmy Kosgei has clapped back at a fan who questioned her why they were yet to have kids with her husband, Anselm Madubuko.

The Taunet Nelel hitmaker had taken to Facebook to celebrate her marriage anniversary to her Nigerian pastor husband which she described as "eight years of bliss"

However, Kosgei's fan identified as Betty Bayoti questioned her when she was intending to have children with Madubuko, the general overseer of Revival Ministries.

"Happy anniversary Emmy, just asking, when are you going to get kids? I know you will tell me God is the giver, yes I know but I'm just curious," said Bayoti.

"We have kids and grandchildren already," said Kosgei.

Kosgei and Madabuko are yet to be blessed with a kid.

The religious power couple has been spreading the gospel worldwide, winning back lost souls to Christ.

In a previous interview, Kosgei alias the Nubian queen revealed that she married an older spouse because he understands her music, respects her, and is supportive.

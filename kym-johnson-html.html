Kym Johnson - Age, Family, Bio

Professional dancer know for her work on Dancing with the Stars, appearing on both the Australian and US versions of the show. She's also served as a judged on DWTS in Australia.

Before Fame

She started dancing when she was three years old. She came in second at the 1998 Australian Dancesport Championships.

Trivia

She's paired up with stars like Jerry Springer and Tom Williams on Dancing with the Stars.

Family Life

She married Robert Herjavec in 2016 and the couple welcomed twins named Haven and Hudson in 2018. She'd previously dated former NFL star Hines Ward. 

Associated With

In Season Four of Dancing with the Stars, she teamed up with Joey Fatone.

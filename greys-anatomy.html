Grey's Anatomy Latest News and Updates

The medical drama Grey's Anatomy premiered in 2005 and followed Dr. Meredith Grey, the daughter of renowned surgeon Dr. Ellis Grey as she started her medical career at Seattle Grace (now Grey Sloan Memorial Hospital). Meredith and the team of doctors and interns at the hospital balance saving lives and their own personal lives on the series, which was created by Shonda Rhimes. 

Grey's Anatomy has become one of the longest-running medical shows in T.V. history and has inspired the spin-offs, Private Practice and Station 19.

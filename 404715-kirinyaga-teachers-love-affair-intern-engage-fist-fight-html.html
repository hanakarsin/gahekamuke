Kirinyaga teachers in love affair with intern engage in fist fight

- Karoti Girls’ High School staffroom became a battlefield as two male teachers confronted each other in a fight over a young female colleague

- The bitter rivalry over the woman took an ugly turn when the other teacher reached for a panga and allegedly slashed his colleague in the head, arms and fingers

- The officer commanding Mwea East police division confirmed the incident, which left the school community in utter shock

A nasty fight broke out at a school in Kirinyaga county on the afternoon of Wednesday, March 3.

Karoti Girls’ High School staffroom became a battlefield as two male teachers confronted each other in a fight over a young female on teaching practice with whom they allegedly had an affair.

The officer commanding Mwea East police division Daniel Kitavi confirmed the incident that left the school community in utter shock and embarrassed the entire teaching staff.

The two colleague teachers turned love rivals had reportedly been cold to each other, and on Wednesday, March 3, they decided to square it out once and for all right in the staffroom.

The bitter rivalry over a woman took an ugly turn when the other teacher reached for a panga and allegedly slashed his colleague in the head, arms and fingers.

“The two male teachers are in a love affair with one female teacher. We have also recovered the weapon he is said to have used to attack his colleague in the school compound. He will be charged with attempted murder," the officer stated.

Kitavi added that the suspect remained in the school compound after the alleged incident even as his colleagues struggled to offer first aid to the victim.

The officers arrested the suspect and drove him to Wang’uru station for statement recording and further interrogation.

Sometime in 2017, students of a school in Kericho were treated to a WWE-like fight between two women, one a deputy principal, over their school principal.

The two women had reportedly been invited by the principal to the school for a date only for the fight to break out.

The two ladies accused each other of ruining the other's relationship, and their shouts escalated into fisticuffs.

It got worse when one of the fighters overpowered the other (the deputy principal) and tore her dress, leaving her with the suit she was born with.

Hailie Jade - Age, Family, Bio

Daughter of rap legend Eminem and Kim Scott. She has over 3.2 million followers on her hailiejade Instagram account. She has been represented by Viral Nation Talent and has posted a variety of fashion, fitness and lifestyle content on her Instagram page. 

Before Fame

Her father worked sixty hours per week during the six months after her birth. 

Trivia

Her father's album Infinite contained references to the struggles he experienced while raising her. He also named "Hailie's Song" after her and put the track on his The Eminem Show album. She began a podcast called Just A Little Shady in 2022.

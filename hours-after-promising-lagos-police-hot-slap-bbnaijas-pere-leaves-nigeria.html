Hours After Promising Lagos Police Hot Slap, BBNaija's Pere Leaves Nigeria

Former BBNaija housemate Pere Egbi has left Nigeria hours after promising to slap a police/mopol officer.

Recall that Naija News earlier reported that Pere took to his Insta Story to explain that some officers are not aware of the reason they are in the force, hence they need to be taught a lesson.

He insisted that he simply needs one officer to teach a lesson that could serve as a warning to others.

He wrote: “One day I go slap mopol/police for this Lagos. Dem just dey misbehave. Just one I need to teach a lesson. Some if them don’t know why they are in the force.”

However it seems the BBNaija star will be postponing his decision to teach the Nigerian police a lesson as he has announced via Instagram that he has arrived in Dubai.

Pere recently reacted to reports that the popular TV show, BBNaija form is for sale.

Speaking on the audition, Pere explained that the auditions forms are free and urged Nigerians to stop asking him the costs involved.

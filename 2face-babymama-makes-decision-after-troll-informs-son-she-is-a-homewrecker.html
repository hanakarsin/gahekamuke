2face Babymama Makes Decision After Troll Informs Son She Is A Homewrecker

Perosaiyemi Adeniyi has decided to look for a troll who sent a message to her son, Justin, tagging her a homewrecker.

The mother of four took to her Insta story to tell the troll that she would never know peace and she would make sure she finds her.

This is coming after Pero slammed 2face wife, Annie Idibia with a N500m lawsuit after Annie accused her of having an affair with her husband.

In the lawsuit, filed on November 10, 2021, Pero’s lawyers stated that Annie’s outburst on Instagram and her allegations that her husband had left their home to be with her in America were false while maintaining that due to these allegations their client and her kids were being bullied online.

Meanwhile, a man has taken to his social media account to reveal how his friend is planning to divorce his wife after a DNA test revealed that their second son is not biologically his.

The man identified as Igwe U Achebe took to his Twitter account to reveal how his friend’s wife got pregnant for a married man she was dating while she was single.

Achebe stated that his friend who was based in Turkey came to Nigeria to marry an Abuja-based medical doctor.

He further explained that the second son looked like the married man and they went ahead to conduct a DNA test which revealed that the man is the boy’s biological father.

The tweet read ”A friend of Mine who came back from Turkey married a Medical Doctor based in Abuja. The girl was dating a married man before getting married.

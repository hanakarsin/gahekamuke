Ahvianna Lee - Age, Family, Bio

Social media personality who shot to stardom as one of the children on The Lee Family YouTube channel. Her family posts a mix of pranks and challenges for more than 360,000 subscribers. She has also posted her own prank and GRWM videos on her Ahvi LeeXO YouTube channel. They began posting more YouTube shorts in 2022.

Before Fame

One of the first recordings she appeared in was 2018's FLUFFY SLIME RECIPE WITH OUR MOM!!

Trivia

She was featured in the 2019 clip AHVI GOT BEAT UP PRANK ON DAD!!! *GETS HEATED*. One of the most viewed videos on her YouTube channel featured her fake crying with the door locked to make her parents worried. 

Family Life

Her parents' names are Georgia and Quinn. She has brothers named Quinn Jr. and Ahmir.

Associated With

On Instagram, she's been pictured alongside fellow YouTubers Yaya Panton and Karissa Eve.

Get the bag: Diamond Platnumz flashes huge bundles of cash in bed

- The Bongo Flava star is known for his flashy lifestyle and does not shy away from showing it off

- The singer went on Instagram sharing short videos as he was chilling in bed with bundles of cash spread all over it

Tanzanian Singer Diamond Platnumz is living the big life as his music continues to top chats in East Africa and Africa at large.

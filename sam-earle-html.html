Sam Earle - Age, Family, Bio

Canadian television actor who played K.C. Guthrie in the teen drama series, Degrassi: The Next Generation. He also starred in the TV movie Stick and Stones.

Before Fame

He acted in short films called Little Holland and Almost Tragic.

Trivia

He attended the University of Toronto after graduating from the same city's Etobicoke School of the Arts.

Family Life

He grew up with a younger sister named Lucy. His father, Chris, is an actor and playwright. His mother Shari is an actor and theater director. 

Associated With

He became close friends with his Degrassi cast mate, Luke Bilyk.

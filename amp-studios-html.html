Amp World - Members, Ages, Trivia

Digital content studio known for partnering with popular creators such as Brent Rivera and Lexi Hensler. The content that they help produce generates over a billion views per month. The group also runs a popular YouTube channel called Amp World as well as an Instagram account under the username ampwrld. The group's YouTube channel has amassed over 5 million subscribers. 

Trivia

They have been involved in unique advertising campaigns, including an innovative and interactive campaign for the 2019 film Countdown. They have also done partnerships with the mobile game Best Fiends and the restaurant chain Chipotle.  In 2021 they announced that they are doing over 1 billion combined views per month across YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and other social platforms.

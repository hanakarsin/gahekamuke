Frankie Just Gym It in mourning after losing beloved grandmother

- Frankie mourned his grandmother as a woman who played a pivotal role in his upbringing

- The Youtuber shared a video on Instagram, spending time with his grandma and young son while helping feed her

- The fitness trainer said his heart was heavy but comforted by the thought of her watching over him from above

An emotional Frankie shared the sad news in a video on YouTube in which he appeared broken and speechless due to the huge loss.

In the short clip dated Sunday, March 7, the fitness enthusiast noted he had just learnt about her passing adding she played a huge part in his upbringing.

The father of three was unable to continue shooting the clip but on the following day, he shared a lovely video on his Instagram while hanging out with her.

In the short clip, the two were in a room as he recorded the granny playfully interacting with his son and towards the end even assisted in feeding her as they had lunch.

Frankie captioned the video:

"My heart is heavy. As you’ve watched over me from birth, I know you’ll continue doing so from above. Rest well Koko. I love you."

Fans and fellow celebrities shared their messages as they consoled the fitness trainer's family.

In other saddening news, TUKO.co.ke earlier reported that KTN News anchor Ken Mijungu is in mourning after the sudden passing of his sister.

The journalist shared an emotional text alongside a video of him and the late sister who was dancing at a party while he held the selfie camera, which showed how close the two were.

In the post, Mijungu said her sudden death had hit him harder than that of his dad.

He revealed the nickname he had given her, noting she was no more and even wondered where baby sisters go when they leave so suddenly.

He wrote:

"This one hit me harder than my dad's death! I called her Jaber inside, she is no more. When your baby sisters leave so sudden, where do they go?"

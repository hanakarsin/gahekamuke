Micahcow - Age, Family, Bio

Short form comedy and lip sync content creator who is recognized for sharing interesting transition videos on his micahcow TikTok account. He has gone on to garner more than 5 million fans on the platform. 

Before Fame

One of his oldest archived TikToks was a transition video titled "Chase the Money." 

Trivia

He parodied the dances that girls do on TikTok. He has had a paid partnership with Hollister Co. and has posted multiple advertisements for them on his micahcow24 Instagram account. 

Family Life

He is in a relationship with fellow TikTok star Ciana Malark.

Associated With

He created a duet video with fellow TikTok creator Jack Bartholow.

Trey Traylor - Age, Family, Bio

Internet star and singer who performs alongside his brother Armon Warren. They have earned over 2.1 million followers on their Ar'mon And Trey Instagram account. They previously garnered a significant following on Vine and they also share YouTube, Twitter and YouNow accounts. 

Before Fame

He was born and raised in Detroit, Michigan. 

Trivia

A phone case that features pictures of Armon and Trey became available in the online store Thug Ave. 

Family Life

His mother's name is Shiquita Hathaway. He and Armon have a total of six siblings, including a brother named Dwight Burton who plays basketball. He welcomed a daughter named Treu with Senia Marie in December 2020. 

Associated With

He and Armon covered Fetty Wap's "Trap Queen" in a six second clip for Vine.

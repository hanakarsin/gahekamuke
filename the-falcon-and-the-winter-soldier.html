'The Falcon and the Winter Soldier Latest News and Updates

Based on the Marvel characters Sam Wilson (Falcon) and Bucky Barnes (Winter Soldier), the limited television series The Falcon and Winter Soldier picks up six months after Sam was handed the mantle of Captain America at the end of Avengers: Endgame.

Anthony Mackie and Sebastian Stan reprise their roles as Sam and Bucky. The Falcon and Winter Soldier shares continuity with films in the Marvel franchise and will run for six episodes. Part of Phase Four of the MCU, the show is joined by other Marvel television series including WandaVision, Loki, and Ms. Marvel, among others. 

Network: Disney+

First Episode Date: March 19, 2021

Cast:

  • Sebastian Stan (Bucky Barnes / Winter Soldier)
  • Anthony Mackie (Sam Wilson / Falcon)
  • Wyatt Russell (John Walker / Captain America)
  • Erin Kellyman (Karli Morgenthau)
  • Daniel Brühl (Helmut Zemo)
  • Emily VanCamp (Sharon Carter)
  • Florence Kasumba (Ayo)
  • Danny Ramirez (Joaquin Torres)
  • Georges St. Pierre (Georges Batroc)
  • Adepero Oduye (Sarah Wilson)
  • Don Cheadle (James Rhodes)
  • Julia Louis-Dreyfus (Valentina Allegra de Fontaine)
  • Clé Bennett (Lemar Hoskins / Battlestar)

Where to Watch: Disney+

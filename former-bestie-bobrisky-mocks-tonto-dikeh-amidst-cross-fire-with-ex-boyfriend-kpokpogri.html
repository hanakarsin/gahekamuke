Former Bestie, Bobrisky Mocks Tonto Dikeh Amidst Cross Fire With Ex Boyfriend, Kpokpogri

Former best friend of Nollywood actress, Tonto Dikeh, Bobrisky has taken to social media to mock her following her recent crossfire, with her ex-lover, Prince Kpokpogri.

Naija News understands that Prince Joseph Kpokpogri recently sued his ex-girlfriend and the Department of State Service(DSS) for allegedly infringing on his basic human rights.

This is coming after the mother of one called him a blackmailer who has several nude videos of his former lovers.

The actress and her former boyfriend had gone their separate ways after a voice note alleged to be Kpokpogri’s voice was heard badmouthing the ‘unrepentant behaviour’ of the actress.

In the lawsuit, Kpokpogri asked the Federal high court in Abuja to stop the DSS from inviting him.

In which the actress replied asking he come forward and answer to his crimes if truly he was innocent as claimed.

Reacting to the back and forth public display of her relationship crisis on social media, Bobrisky mocked her, saying she keeps disgracing herself everywhere. 

His post reads; “If you no get data dis September ehhh.. I am enjoying Aunty disgracing herself everywhere”

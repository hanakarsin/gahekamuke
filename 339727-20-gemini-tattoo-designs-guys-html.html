20 Gemini tattoo designs for guys

Gemini is part of the larger Zodiac family. Like other Zodiac groupings, Gemini is synonymous with signs, and they all try to explain life. One of the best ways to embody these signs is by getting a tattoo. If you have decided to get inked, it is vital to understand that all Gemini tattoo designs have meanings.

In Astrology, Gemini is one of the masculine air signs whose symbol is twins. According to the Greek mythology where this sign originates from, Gemini represents twin brothers, Castor and Pollux, who shared the same mother but different fathers.

Castor’s father, Tyndareus, was mortal while Pollux’ father, Zeus, was a god hence immortal. Later in their lives, Castor was killed and in sorrow, Pollux asked his father, Zeus, to grant his dead brother immortality so that he could be saved from death. Zeus eventually agreed and the twin brothers formed the Gemini constellation.

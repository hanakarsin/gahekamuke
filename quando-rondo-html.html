Quando Rondo - Age, Family, Bio

Rapper whose single "Motivation" was featured on the World Star Hip Hop YouTube channel and has racked up over three million views.  

Before Fame

His stage name comes from people calling him "Ty-Quando" as a kid. He kept the "Quando" and added "Rondo" because he liked the way it sounded. 

Trivia

He has over 3.6 million followers on Instagram. 

Family Life

He is from Savannah, Georgia and was born Tyquian Terrel Bowman. He had a daughter named Italy with his then-girlfriend Jai. 

Associated With

His single "I Remember" features rapper Lil Baby.

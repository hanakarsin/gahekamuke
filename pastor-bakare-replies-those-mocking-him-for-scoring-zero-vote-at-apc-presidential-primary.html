Pastor Bakare Replies Those Mocking Him For Scoring Zero Vote At APC Presidential Primary

The general overseer of Citadel Community Global Church better known as Latter Rain Assembly, Pastor Tunde Bakare has replied those mocking his performance at the just concluded presidential primary election of the All Progressives Congress, APC.

Naija News reports that the All Progressives Congress, APC, under which the General Overseer of The Citadel Global Community Church contested the 2023 presidential race lasted through Wednesday, June 8, which led the party’s national leader, Bola Ahmed Tinubu clinching the 2023 presidential ticket to battle oppositions in the general elections.

Reacting to the critics, the cleric in a social media post on Wednesday said he was happy because he was different while saying those who were desirous of effecting a change must be different.

He wrote, “I am happy I passed the test of character by not paying any delegate.

“I passed the test of courage by speaking the truth in the midst of wolves and the test of conviction by standing true to my beliefs. Those who will make a difference must be different.”

Recall, Bakare told his congregation in one of his Sunday services, that President Muhammadu Buhari is number 15 and he would succeed him as number 16 president.

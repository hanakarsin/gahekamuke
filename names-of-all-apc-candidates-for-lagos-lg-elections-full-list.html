Names Of All APC Candidates For Lagos LG Elections [Full List]

The Lagos State chapter of the All Progressives Congress (APC) has revealed its candidates for the July 24 local council elections in the state.

The council election will be held into the 20 Local Government Councils and the 37 Local Council Development Areas (LCDAs) of the state.

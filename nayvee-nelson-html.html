NayVee Nelson - Age, Family, Bio

One of 16 children featured on the popular YouTube channel Not Enough Nelsons that includes skits, challenges, vlogs and more. They have earned more than 1.3 million subscribers. Nayvee was featured in an April 2019 YouTube video entitled "ROOM TOUR!!! Surprise Makeover for NAYVEE!" where she picked out new design items for her bedroom. 

Before Fame

Her family debuted on YouTube with a video called "15 SIBLINGS?! I'M NO LONGER A PART OF THIS FAMILY!"

Trivia

Her video "ROOM TOUR!!! Surprise Makeover for NAYVEE!" received over 1.6 million views on YouTube. Her instagram has included multiple dance and lip-sync videos to popular songs. 

Family Life

Her siblings are Ledger, Beckham, ElleCee, PresLee Grace, DeLayNee June, PaisLee, Luke, Trey, SaiDee, LiLee, KassaDee, JourNee, ElleCee, and KennaDee. Her name prior to adoption was Unique Destiny.

Associated With

Her family collaborated with Shae Bennett and the rest of The Ohana Adventure in a video called ""24 HOURS OVERNIGHT HOUSE SWAP! | WITH The Ohana Adventure!"

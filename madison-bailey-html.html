Madison Bailey - Age, Family, Bio

Actress best known for her role as Kiara on the Netflix show Outer Banks. She has appeared on several other television shows including Black Lightning and Creepshow. 

Before Fame

Her first acting role was on the show Constantine. 

Trivia

She plays the ukelele. 

Family Life

She is the youngest of seven siblings. Her sister has appeared as her stunt double on Outer Banks. She began dating Mariah Linney in 2020.

Associated With

She and Chase Stokes both appear on the show Outer Banks.

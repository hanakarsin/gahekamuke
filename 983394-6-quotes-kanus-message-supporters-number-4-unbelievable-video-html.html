6 things Nnamdi Kanu told to supporters in court (video)

Just after the judge presiding over the criminal matter against him made his ruling, Nnamdi Kanu addressed his relatives and supporters who were able to make it into the courtroom today, September 26.

Speaking to his supporters, while seated on one of the chairs in the courtroom, Kanu in a defiant tone said: "What we are fighting for is corruption." 

The judge, Justice John Tsoho, who was presiding over criminal charges bordering on treasonable felony among others against Kanu, has earlier today disqualified himself from the matter.
